The land use and zoning for most blocks are housing, services, institutions, and leisure purposes.

The distribution of the commercial area largely overlaps with the Finger Plan and major transportation routes.

A New Island: Refshaleoen

There is a rising island on the east of Copenhagen that you might want to take a look.

There are lots of art studios, cafes and informal creative activities on this island. The government is planning to put 1.5 million square meters building area on this island. The island is still expanding towards the north.
